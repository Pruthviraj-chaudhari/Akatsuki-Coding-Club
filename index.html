About Us

Akatsuki is a coding club created by students of TY COMP in 2021, the creation of this club has been in the works ever since from our First Year in the college and finally with a lot of help and support from Faculty Members and Juniors we were able to make it a reality. This club aspires to help students across the Institute to become the best versions of themselves and develop their skillset while having a place to consult with their Seniors and Alumni.

MISSION & VISION

"Of the Students For the Student and By the Students"

1.

Our mission is to estabilish a coding culture in our students and inculcate the coding habits necessary to crack Product based companies.

2.

To spark a flame of innovation and positive change in the students, giving them all the necessary tools to tranform their lives and achieve a bright future.

3.

To cultivate curiosity, encourage experimentation, and prepare students for success in an interconnected world through a transdisciplinary, non-hierarchical learning environment.

4.

To inspire confidence and curiosity by eliminating coding phobia from students, fostering a passion for coding and problem-solving.
